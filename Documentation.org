* Motors

- Activate a motor:
#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
 digitalWrite(MOTOR_ENABLE,HIGH)
#+END_EXAMPLE
- Deactivate a motor:
#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
 digitalWrite(MOTOR_ENABLE,LOW)
#+END_EXAMPLE
- Make a motor go forward:
#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
 analogWrite(MOTOR_PHASE,0)
#+END_EXAMPLE
- Make a motor go backward:
#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
 analogWrite(MOTOR_PHASE,255)
#+END_EXAMPLE
- Make a motor stop:
#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
 analogWrite(MOTOR_PHASE,128)
#+END_EXAMPLE
